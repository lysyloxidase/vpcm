# FDA-EMA Good AI Practice Cross-Reference

Source checked May 17, 2026: FDA page and PDF for "Guiding Principles of Good
AI Practice in Drug Development", January 2026.

VPCM alignment to the 10 principles:

1. Human-centric by design: restricted COU and clinician-override language.
2. Risk-based approach: High influence x Moderate consequence -> Significant.
3. Adherence to standards: ASME V&V 40 and FDA seven-step mapping.
4. Clear Context of Use: `docs/COU.md` and signed model card.
5. Multidisciplinary expertise: biology, ML, causal, clinical, and regulatory
   evidence sections in the dossier.
6. Data governance and documentation: provenance, DVC hashes, audit logs.
7. Model design and development practices: frozen FMs, LoRA traceability,
   deterministic CI, and baseline controls.
8. Risk-based performance assessment: prospective blinded benchmark thresholds.
9. Lifecycle management: quarterly monitoring and annual atlas refresh.
10. Clear essential information: signed model card, report bundle, and Type C
    briefing questions.
