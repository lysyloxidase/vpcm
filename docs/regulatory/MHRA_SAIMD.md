# MHRA Software and AI as a Medical Device Cross-Reference

Source checked May 17, 2026: MHRA Software and AI as a Medical Device Change
Programme roadmap, updated June 14, 2023, and MHRA software/AI as medical
device overview updated February 3, 2025.

VPCM alignment:

- Intended purpose is explicitly bounded in `docs/COU.md`.
- AI-specific rigor is addressed by refusal gates, conformal uncertainty,
  prospective external validation, and transparent baseline comparisons.
- Interpretability is supported through pathway, GRN, communication, and
  caveated attention reports.
- Adaptivity is controlled: foundation models are frozen, patient adapters are
  audited, and lifecycle changes require re-validation.
- Post-market style controls are represented as quarterly monitoring and
  recalibration requirements.
