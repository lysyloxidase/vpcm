# NMPA AI Medical Device Cross-Reference

Source checked May 17, 2026: NMPA English regulations portal and public
summaries of 2022 artificial-intelligence medical-device registration
guidelines.

VPCM alignment:

- Registration-style technical documentation: V&V 40 dossier, model card,
  provenance manifest, and signed prediction reports.
- Algorithm transparency: mechanism reports, baseline tables, and caveated
  attention attributions.
- Dataset representativeness: dataset inventory and fairness-audit hooks.
- Performance evaluation: prospective blinded benchmark and Section 8 gates.
- Change control: frozen v1.0.0 tag, lifecycle monitoring, and re-validation
  triggers.
