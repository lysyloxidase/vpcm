# EMA AI Medicines Regulation Cross-Reference

Source checked May 17, 2026: EMA reflection paper
`EMA/CHMP/CVMP/83833/2023`, first published September 30, 2024.

VPCM alignment:

- Clear Context of Use: clinical-trial enrichment, repurposing hypotheses, and
  biomarker strategy planning only.
- Data governance: patient hashes, provenance manifests, DVC/Lamin-style
  records, and signed audit logs.
- Bias controls: ancestry-stratified PRS handling and cell-type representation
  audits.
- Lifecycle controls: quarterly drift monitoring, quarterly conformal
  recalibration, and annual atlas refresh.
- Human oversight: no diagnosis, no dosing, no primary endpoint replacement,
  and no treatment decision without clinician override.
